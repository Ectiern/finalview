Where it all started..

I was lucky enough to grow up in family where my mom made dinner every single night. Due to my sisters and I having Celiac Disease, eating out was a struggle for a long time. My mom is amazing and took this as an opportunity to become an amazing cook! She has taught me everything I know about the kitchen, and continues to take my calls pleading for help still! Around middle school, as my sisters and I got older, we began to experiment with baking. This became our obsession for a long time and together we learned how to bake delicious gluten free baked goods. Now, I find that baking is a big stress reliever for me, as it is a task that requires a lot of concentration. I also love to bake for my family and friends when they need something to cheer them up!


Where I am now!

I still love to bake! I have a few blogs I check frequently and I am always excited to try out a new recipe. Two of my roommates this year are vegan, so I have really enjoyed experimenting with gluten free and vegan baking! I think the concept sounds more intimidating than it really is, and I promise it still tastes great!
